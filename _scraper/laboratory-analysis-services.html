Why Choose Us.

Quality Laboratory Services

  • Trusted expertise

    Cropnuts is one of the few  ISO/IEC 17025 Kenas accredited calibration laboratories in East Africa. We’ve been accredited for close to 10 years now with a scope of 11 laboratory tests under the ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation

    .

  • Rapid turnaround times

    We take pride in having one of the fastest laboratory turn around times in the region. Our qualified analysis team and streamlined operations delight our customers with quick and reliable laboratory results.

  • Smart Advice

    Our professional, user friendly laboratory reports and data-driven recommendations enable you to make the best management decisions for improving crop yields, crop quality, water quality, food safety and the environment (click link to view our success stories)

  • Customer support

    We pride ourselves on having close working relationships with our clients, and all our services are backed up by our customer services and technical support. We operate with complete confidentiality in all aspects of our work.

Our Laboratory is Internationally Accredited

Cropnuts is ISO/IEC 17025 accredited by the Kenya Accreditation Service (KENAS). ISO/IEC 17025 accreditation fulfills the general competence requirements for testing and calibration laboratories and demonstrates our ability to carry out laboratory testing and calibration to an international standard.
